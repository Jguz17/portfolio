About

I'm an ambitious dev with a thirst for knowledge, and I like finding new ways to solve simple/complex problems. I have a little over 3 years of experience working as a Software Engineer and I like to make cool things on the web :). I've only worked with two companies and you can find a little more about them below:

Hungr

Hungr is a startup that focuses on food delivery, kind of like Uber Eats. While I was here, I worked with a team that was in charge of building out the mobile app that took the orders of the user, processed it, and then set it out for delivery. We were a team of 5 devs, but I specifically worked on the login feature. We collaberated with the project manager and designers.

Zenyx

Zenyx is a digital agency that I worked with for about 2 years. We worked with a lot of clients and built really cool apps and websites! My favorite thing that I built is an app for grocery stores that functioned as a POS and inventory system. The company focused on saas, so we built software for businesses that needed to ease their workload.
