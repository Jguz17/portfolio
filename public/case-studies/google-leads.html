Google Leads

Designed to help find small business info.

Something to help myself

I am building this project to help me, and hopefully other people find snippets of information from nearby businesses.

I'm still in the process of finishing up this project! As of now, the only feature that works right is creating and deleting your own "place", which is saved in your "my places" section. Another feature that works is searching up businesses - when a user enters their desried location they will be shown of nearby businesses. However, sometimes you won't be able to receive any data because Google has a limit to how many requests you can make to their Places API. If this happens, just wait a couple minutes or check back at a later time!
